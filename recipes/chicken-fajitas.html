Chicken Fajitas

Description

In this chicken fajitas recipe, the chicken is infused with a flavourful marinade, then cooked with bell peppers, onions and garlic. Served with your favourite tortillas, guacamole, and homemade salsa, these chicken fajitas are an easy, healthy & delicious week night meal.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Chicken Breasts
  • Lime Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Herbs & Spices - cumin, paprika, chilli powder & coriander
  • Mixed Peppers
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Whisk together the lime juice, olive oil and minced garlic in a medium bowl
  2. Next, add the chicken and stir to coat
  3. In a small bowl combine spices and stir to combine
  4. Next, add the spices and turn to coat
  5. Cover the bowl and marinate chicken for at least 1 hour at room temperature
  6. Drain excess liquid from the marinated chicken.
  7. Heat one tablespoon of olive oil in a large cast iron skillet until shimmering
  8. Next, add the chicken, onions and garlic and cook over medium-high heat until chicken is browned, stirring occasionally
  9. Then, add the peppers and another tablespoon of olive oil
  10. Cook over high heat until seared/browned (3-5 minutes). If necessary, add more olive oil to brown the fajitas
  11. Serve with tortillas, guacamole and salsa
